## 2. Reel/Short-form Video Ideas

1.  **The Inversion Test (15 sec):** Practitioner does a fast flow into a handstand. Text overlay: "Does your tank stay put?" Show the LotusTank staying perfectly in place. Music: High-energy, upbeat beat.
2.  **Fabric Performance (20 sec):** Fast-paced close-ups of the technical features: 4-way stretch, moisture-wicking (show water beading), and anti-odor labels. Ending with a "Designed for Hot Yoga" stamp.
3.  **Get Ready With Me - Yoga Edition (30 sec):** Influencer picks their LotusTank color, pairs it with leggings, and rolls out their mat. Simple, aesthetic, lifestyle focus.
4.  **The "8 Limbs" Design Story (15 sec):** Macro shots of the geometric lotus pattern on the side panels. Voiceover or text explaining the 8 interlocking petals for the 8 limbs of yoga.
5.  **Studio to Street (25 sec):** Transition video from a yoga class (wearing the tank) to a coffee shop (tank paired with a light jacket/denim). Shows versatility.

## 4. Engagement Plan

**Daily Actions (The "Power 30"):**
- **Community Outreach:** Spend 15 mins daily commenting on posts from 10 relevant yoga accounts (influencers, studios, practitioners). Focus on meaningful, supportive comments, not just emojis.
- **Story Interaction:** Reply to every story mention and tag. Reshare UGC concepts to our own stories to build community feel.
- **Waitlist Nurture:** DM new waitlist sign-ups a quick "Welcome to the flow!" message to build personal connection.
- **Polls & Q&As:** Use Instagram Stories daily to run polls (e.g., "Sage or Clay?") or Q&A sessions about the fabric and fit.
