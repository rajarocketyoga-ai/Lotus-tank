# LotusTank Brand Voice & Taglines

## Tagline Options
1. **"Rooted in Practice. Flow in Motion."** (Primary)
2. **"The Bloom of Your Practice."**
3. **"Premium Performance for the Modern Yogi."**

## Brand Voice Guidelines
- **Serene & Grounded**: Use language that evokes a sense of calm and stability. Avoid hype-heavy "gym" marketing.
- **Knowledgeable**: Speak with authority on fabric tech and yoga tradition. We are "the teacher's brand."
- **Minimalist**: Clear, concise communication. Let the quality and design speak for itself.
- **Inclusive & Empowering**: Welcome practitioners of all levels while maintaining a premium positioning.
