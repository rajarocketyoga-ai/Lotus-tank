# LotusTank Launch Collection: Product Spec Sheet

## 1. General Collection Specs
*   **Fabric Technology**: All tanks feature LotusDry™ technology (moisture-wicking, 4-way stretch, anti-odor).
*   **Primary Material**: 78% Recycled Nylon, 22% Spandex (for Sage and Charcoal models) or 80% Recycled Polyester, 20% Spandex (for Cream and Multi-color models).
*   **Care Instructions**: 
    *   Machine wash cold with like colors.
    *   Do not bleach.
    *   Tumble dry low or hang dry (recommended for longevity).
    *   Do not iron.
