# LotusTank Product Concepts

1. **The "Drishti" Racerback**
   - **Design**: High-neck, narrow racerback for maximum shoulder mobility.
   - **Graphic**: A tiny, tonal embroidered third-eye symbol at the nape of the neck.
   - **Fabric**: Ultra-wicking Sage Green fabric.

2. **The "Eight Limbs" Panel Tank**
   - **Design**: Standard fit with breathable mesh side panels.
   - **Graphic**: A geometric pattern of 8 interlocking lotus petals on the side panels.
   - **Fabric**: Charcoal body with Terracotta mesh accents.

3. **The "Breath Wave" Flow Tank**
   - **Design**: Loose, breezy cut with a dropped hem for coverage during inversions.
   - **Graphic**: Subtle, tonal wave patterns on the lower back representing Prana (breath).
   - **Fabric**: Warm Cream moisture-wicking blend.

4. **The "Lotus Core" Essential**
   - **Design**: Tight, compressive fit with a built-in shelf bra.
   - **Graphic**: A small, gold-foil LotusTank logo centered just below the back neckline.
   - **Fabric**: Deep Sage Green with Soft Gold accents.
