# LotusTank: Printful Tank Top Research

## 1. Top 3 Tank Styles for Hot Yoga

### 1.1 Sport-Tek Women's PosiCharge® Competitor™ Racerback Tank (ST390)
*   **Unit Cost**: ~$14.95 - $16.50
*   **Fabric Composition & Weight**: 100% polyester interlock (3.8 oz/yd²). Features PosiCharge technology to lock in color and prevent bleeding.
*   **Available Colors (Lotus Palette)**: 
    *   **Silver** (Light neutral alternative to Cream)
    *   **Iron Grey** (Charcoal equivalent)
    *   **Deep Sage** (Matches Brand Sage Green)
*   **Sizes Offered**: XS–4XL
*   **Print Quality**: Best for **Embroidery** or high-quality **DTG** (Direct-to-Garment). Interlock fabric provides a smooth surface.
*   **Best Use Case**: High-intensity Vinyasa and Hot Yoga where moisture-wicking and quick-dry performance are the top priorities.
*   **Sample Order Cost**: ~$12.00 (plus shipping)

### 1.2 Bella + Canvas Women's Flowy Racerback Tank (8802)
*   **Unit Cost**: ~$11.50 - $13.00
*   **Fabric Composition & Weight**: 65% polyester, 35% viscose (3.7 oz/yd²). Flowy, draped silhouette.
*   **Available Colors (Lotus Palette)**:
    *   **Sage** (Direct match)
    *   **Dark Grey Heather** (Charcoal equivalent)
    *   **Soft Pink / Cream** (Warm Cream alternative)
*   **Sizes Offered**: XS–2XL
*   **Print Quality**: Excellent for **DTG**. The poly-viscose blend creates a "vintage" look with prints.
*   **Best Use Case**: Lifestyle yoga, gentle flows, and "Studio to Street" wear. Preferred by customers who value softness and style.
*   **Sample Order Cost**: ~$9.50 (plus shipping)

### 1.3 Next Level Apparel Women's Ideal Racerback (6733)
*   **Unit Cost**: ~$10.50 - $12.00
*   **Fabric Composition & Weight**: 60% combed ringspun cotton, 40% polyester (4.0 oz/yd²).
*   **Available Colors (Lotus Palette)**:
    *   **Military Green** (Sage Green equivalent)
    *   **Heavy Metal** (Charcoal equivalent)
    *   **Desert Pink** (Terracotta/Clay alternative)
*   **Sizes Offered**: XS–2XL
*   **Print Quality**: High quality **DTG**. The cotton blend allows for vibrant, sharp prints.
*   **Best Use Case**: The best "all-rounder." Good for practitioners who prefer the feel of cotton but need the durability and slight wicking of polyester.
*   **Sample Order Cost**: ~$8.50 (plus shipping)

---

## 2. Final Recommendation

**Primary Recommendation: Next Level Apparel 6733 (The Ideal Racerback)**
*   **Why**: It offers the best balance of price ($10.50), color variety (Military Green and Heavy Metal are perfect for LotusTank), and fabric feel. It's a crowd favorite for yoga brands due to its flattering fit and durability.

**Performance Alternative: Sport-Tek ST390**
*   **Why**: For the "Performance Line" specifically marketed for hot yoga, the 100% polyester interlock is superior for moisture management. We should offer this as a premium $55 "Tech Tank" while using the Next Level 6733 for our standard $48 designs.
