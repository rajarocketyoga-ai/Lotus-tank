# LotusTank Brand Identity Guide

## 1. Brand Vision
LotusTank provides premium, performance-driven yoga apparel designed for the heat of practice. We blend minimalist aesthetics with high-performance fabric technology.

## 2. Logo
The LotusTank logo is a stylized, minimalist lotus flower. It represents growth, purity, and the "bloom" of a dedicated practice.
- **Logo Mark:** /home/team/shared/brand/logo_mark.png
- **Concepts:** /home/team/shared/brand/logo_concepts.png

## 3. Color Palette
Our colors are inspired by nature, grounding the practitioner while maintaining a premium feel.

## 4. Brand Voice & Tagline
- **Tagline:** "Rooted in Practice. Flow in Motion."
- **Brand Voice:** 
  - **Calm but Empowering:** We speak with the quiet confidence of a seasoned teacher.
  - **Knowledgeable:** We understand the physics of an inversion and the chemistry of moisture-wicking fabric.
  - **Minimalist:** We say more with less. No fluff, just performance.

## 5. Product Design Concepts
Subtle, philosophically inspired designs.

1. **The "Drishti" Tank:** A high-neck racerback with a single, tiny embroidered eye at the nape of the neck to represent focus.
2. **The "Eight Limbs" Series:** A series of tanks featuring a geometric lotus petal pattern on the side panel, symbolizing the path of yoga.
3. **The "Inversion" Essential:** A longer-cut tank with a silicone-grip hem inside to ensure it stays in place during downward dog and handstands. Subtle tonal "LotusTank" branding.
4. **The "Prana" Gradient:** A moisture-wicking tank with a subtle ombre effect from bottom to top, representing the rising of energy.
