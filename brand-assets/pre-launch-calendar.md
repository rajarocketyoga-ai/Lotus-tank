# Pre-Launch Marketing Plan

## Target Audience
- **Primary:** Female yoga practitioners (22-45) who practice hot yoga or vinyasa 2-5x per week.
- **Interests:** Sustainable fashion, mindfulness, high-performance activewear, boutique fitness.

## Channels
1. **Instagram (Primary):** High-aesthetic visuals, Reels for performance demonstration, and Stories for engagement.
2. **TikTok (Secondary):** Raw, "Behind the Scenes" content, yoga tips, and trend-based videos.
3. **Email (Conversion):** Driving sign-ups to the waitlist for exclusive early access and "Launch Day" discounts.

## Growth Tactics
- **Waitlist Incentive:** Join the list to get a "Launch Day" secret password and 15% off your first order.
- **Giveaway (Day 9):** Partner with 1-2 micro-influencers (10k-50k followers) in the yoga space to co-host a giveaway to expand reach.
- **Countdowns:** Daily Story countdown stickers to create FOMO.

## Success Metrics (KPIs)
- **Waitlist Subscribers:** Target 500+ before Day 14.
- **Instagram Engagement Rate:** Target 3%+.
- **Video Views (Reels/TikTok):** Target 2,000+ per video.
