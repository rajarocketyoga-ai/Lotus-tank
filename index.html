Our Story

Made for the practice,
not the pose.

LotusTank was born on the mat — literally. After years of practicing hot yoga in tanks that stretched out, rode up, or smelled after one session, we decided to build something better.

Every LotusTank is engineered with premium moisture-wicking fabric, four-way stretch, and anti-odor technology. The cut is designed to stay put through inversions, the fabric breathes through the hottest flows, and the minimalist designs draw inspiration from yoga philosophy.

No logos plastered across your back. No baggy cotton that soaks through. Just you, your practice, and a tank that disappears so you can focus on what matters.
